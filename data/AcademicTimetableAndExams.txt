Q: What is this example file?
A: This is a placeholder example file and does not contain official UNIMA student services information.

Q: What do I do if a course I need is full?
A: Contact your Head of Department. In some cases, class capacity may be increased or an alternative section may be arranged.

Q: What are the official lecture hours at UNIMA?
A: The official periods of operation are from 7:30am to 4:30pm, Monday to Friday. The University also offers weekend courses, and staff and students may arrange lectures or discussions outside these hours.

Q: Can I transfer credits from another institution?
A: Yes, UNIMA has a credit transfer policy. Students should contact the admissions team with their transcripts for evaluation.

Q: when are class timetables released?
A: Class timetables are released before the semester begins.

Q: How do to access class timetables?
A: Log into the student portal then go to the main navigation menu and go to "Timetables"

Q: Do timetables ever change?
A: Timetables may change during the first weeks threfore students should check regularly for updates.

Q: What is the duration of each lecture?
A: Each lecture lasts 1 hours with each lecture having 3 credit hours per week.

Q: When are examinations held?
A: Exams are held at the end of each semester.

Q: What happens if students Miss exams?
A: Students must provide valid reasons with documentation and thereafter wait to seat for deffered exams.
