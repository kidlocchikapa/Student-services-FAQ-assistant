Q: What is the application fee for University of Malawi programmes?
A: The application fee is:
- Malawian Nationals: MWK 46,800
- International Students: USD 30

Q: What is the enrollment fee?
A: The enrollment fee is:
- Malawian Nationals: MWK 315,000
- International Students: USD 250

Q: How much are undergraduate tuition fees for Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Business Management?
A: The undergraduate tuition fees for Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Business Management are charged per month as follows:
- Year 1: Malawian Nationals - MWK 208,800; International Students - USD 165
- Year 2: Malawian Nationals - MWK 208,800; International Students - USD 185
- Year 3: Malawian Nationals - MWK 208,800; International Students - USD 185
- Year 4: Malawian Nationals - MWK 208,800; International Students - USD 185

Q: Is there a technology fee?
A: Yes. The technology fee is charged per subject:
- Malawian Nationals: MWK 19,800 per subject
- International Students: USD 10 per subject

Q: What are the postgraduate tuition fees for master's programmes?
A: The postgraduate tuition fees for master's programmes (MBA / MSc) are charged per month as follows:
- Year 1: MWK 243,000; International Students - USD 190
- Year 2: MWK 261,000; International Students - USD 225

Q: What are the doctorate programme tuition fees?
A: The doctorate programme tuition fees for PhD / DBA programmes are charged per month as follows:
- Year 1: MWK 360,000; International Students - USD 235-245
- Year 2: MWK 378,000; International Students - USD 255-265
- Year 3: MWK 378,000; International Students - USD 255-275

Q: Can fees change?
A: Yes. Tuition and other fees may change without prior notice.

Q: Are repeated subjects charged separately?
A: Yes. Students who repeat subjects are charged additional fees.

Q: How are fees paid?
A: Fees are usually paid monthly or according to the selected payment plan.

Q: What proof is needed for Malawian fee status?
A: Students must provide proof of residency or nationality to qualify for Malawian national fees.

Q: How should the assistant respond when a student asks a broad tuition-fee question?
A: If a student asks a broad question such as "How much are fees at UNIMA?", explain that UNIMA tuition fees vary by programme and student category. Then ask the student for:
- their programme or course
- whether they are undergraduate or postgraduate
- whether they are government-sponsored or self-sponsored
