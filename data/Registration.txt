

Q: When does registration at the University of Malawi (UNIMA) take place?
A: Registration starts in the first week of each semester and runs for a period of two weeks. Students must register within this period to be fully recognized as active students.

Q: How do students register at UNIMA?
A: Registration is done through the UNIMA student portal at https://portal.unima.ac.mw/students/
. Students log in using their username and password and complete the registration process online.

Q: Is UNIMA registration done manually or online?
A: Registration is fully online through the student portal and the system automatically updates student status once payment is confirmed.

Q: What payment is required before registration at UNIMA?
A: Students must pay a minimum of approximately MK11,500 before they are allowed to access and complete registration.

Q: What happens after paying the registration fee at UNIMA?
A: Once payment is confirmed, the system automatically activates registration access within seconds, allowing the student to register through the portal.

Q: What is the UNIMA fee payment structure after registration?
A: Students must pay half of the fees after two weeks and the remaining balance after one month. Failure to complete payments may result in deregistration.

Q: What happens if a student does not pay full fees at UNIMA?
A: If a student fails to complete payment requirements, they may be automatically deregistered without refund.

Q: What happens if a student misses the registration deadline at UNIMA?
A: Students who miss registration may be treated as withdrawn and must wait for the next academic year to apply again.

Q: Can students still attend lectures without registering at UNIMA?
A: Yes, students may physically attend lectures, but they will not be officially registered.

Q: What are the consequences of not registering at UNIMA?
A: Unregistered students cannot access full timetables, are not included in official program lists, and lecturers cannot formally invite them to classes.

Q: Is UNIMA registration approved manually by staff?
A: No. Registration is automatic. Once payment is confirmed, the system immediately allows the student to register without manual approval.

Q: What changes after a student completes registration at UNIMA?
A: Registered students gain full access to their timetable, are included in official class lists, and are fully recognized in academic systems.
